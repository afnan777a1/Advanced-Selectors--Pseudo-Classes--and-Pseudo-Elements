Codingal is an online coding platform that offers live, interactive programming courses for students from grades 1 to 12. It aims to make coding fun and accessible, empowering kids to build apps, games, and websites.

Codingal is a STEM-accredited platform focused on nurturing coding skills in school students through project-based learning. With expert instructors and a global student base, it helps young minds develop future-ready tech skills.
